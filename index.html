Queensland Taiwan Cup Charity Golf Tournament

Connect through golf. Give back through charity.

About the Event

Welcome to the Queensland Taiwan Cup, the premier charity golf event bringing together Taiwanese communities and friends in Queensland. Join us to celebrate sportsmanship, unity, and giving back to society through this unique fusion of golf and charity.

Our Mission

The Queensland Taiwan Cup connects people through golf, supports local charities, and showcases the compassion and positive spirit of the Taiwanese community in Australia.

Event Details

  • Date: October 14, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM Registration | 12:00 PM Shotgun Start

  • Dress Code: Golf attire

Gala Dinner: October 16, 2025 | 5:45 PM Entry | 6:00 PM Start | Landmark Restaurant, Sunnybank Plaza
